MAINTENANCE

Keep Your Computer
Running Like New.

Learn proper cleaning, preventive maintenance, and optimization to improve performance and extend the lifespan of your PC.

Preventive Maintenance

Dust Cleaning

Remove dust from your PC to improve airflow and cooling.

  1. Turn off and unplug the computer.
  2. Open the side panel.
  3. Use compressed air to remove dust.
  4. Clean fans and air vents.
  5. Close the case and reconnect power.

Thermal Paste Replacement

Replace old thermal paste for better CPU cooling.

  1. Remove the CPU cooler.
  2. Clean old thermal paste.
  3. Apply a pea-sized amount of new paste.
  4. Reinstall the cooler.

Fan Cleaning

Keep your fans clean to reduce heat and noise.

  1. Hold the fan blades.
  2. Blow compressed air.
  3. Wipe remaining dust.
  4. Reconnect everything.

Battery Care

Extend your laptop battery's lifespan.

  1. Avoid overheating.
  2. Use the original charger.
  3. Don't keep it plugged in constantly.
  4. Enable battery saver mode.

Storage Cleanup

Free up storage space for better performance.

  1. Delete temporary files.
  2. Uninstall unused software.
  3. Empty the Recycle Bin.
  4. Run Disk Cleanup.

Driver Updates

Keep hardware drivers updated for stability.

  1. Open Device Manager.
  2. Check for outdated drivers.
  3. Download updates from the manufacturer.
  4. Restart your PC.

Monitor Cleaning

Keep your monitor clean for a clear and sharp display.

  1. Turn off the monitor.
  2. Use a microfiber cloth.
  3. Apply screen cleaner to the cloth.
  4. Wipe gently in circular motions.

Cable Management

Organize cables to improve airflow and make upgrades easier.

  1. Bundle cables using zip ties.
  2. Separate power and data cables.
  3. Keep cables away from fans.
  4. Ensure nothing blocks airflow.
